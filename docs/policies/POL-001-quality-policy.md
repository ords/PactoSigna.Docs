# Quality Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes PactoSigna's commitment to quality and regulatory compliance for its Quality Management System (QMS) software product. It defines the quality principles that guide all product development, delivery, and support activities.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all PactoSigna personnel, including software engineers, quality assurance staff, product managers, and leadership. It covers all activities related to the design, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance of the PactoSigna QMS platform.

## 3. Policy Statement

> PactoSigna is committed to delivering a QMS platform that enables medical device companies to achieve and maintain regulatory compliance with confidence. We pursue continuous improvement in product quality, customer satisfaction, and regulatory adherence through disciplined engineering practices and data-driven decision making.

## 4. Definitions

| Term                  | Definition                                                                          |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Quality               | The degree to which PactoSigna meets stated requirements and customer expectations  |
| Continual Improvement | Recurring activity to enhance the QMS and its outputs based on measured performance |
| Customer              | Medical device companies using PactoSigna for quality management                    |
| Regulatory Compliance | Conformity with applicable standards (ISO 13485, IEC 62304, FDA 21 CFR Part 11)     |

## 5. Requirements

### 5.1 Customer Focus

- All product decisions shall consider the impact on customer regulatory compliance
- Customer feedback shall be systematically collected and incorporated into product improvement
- Product features shall be validated against real-world regulatory and audit scenarios

### 5.2 Regulatory Compliance

- The PactoSigna QMS shall conform to ISO 13485, IEC 62304 (Class C), and FDA 21 CFR Part 11
- All design and development activities shall follow documented SOPs
- Compliance shall be verified through internal audits and management reviews

### 5.3 Continual Improvement

- Quality objectives (POL-002) shall be measured and reviewed at defined intervals
- Corrective and preventive actions (CAPA) shall be tracked to closure
- Process performance data shall inform improvement priorities

## 6. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role               | Responsibility                                                           |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Leadership         | Establish quality policy, allocate resources, conduct management reviews |
| QA Manager         | Monitor quality objectives, manage CAPA, conduct internal audits         |
| Product Manager    | Ensure requirements reflect customer and regulatory needs                |
| Software Engineers | Follow SOPs, participate in design reviews, maintain traceability        |
| All Personnel      | Understand and comply with this policy and applicable SOPs               |

## 7. Compliance

Compliance with this policy is monitored through:

- Management reviews (SOP-010) at planned intervals
- Internal audits of QMS processes
- Quality objective tracking and trend analysis (POL-002)

Non-compliance shall be addressed through the CAPA process (SOP-009).
